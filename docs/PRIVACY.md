## What this app is
Minor Illusion is a tabletop-RPG companion. A **Game Master (GM)** runs a web
console that orchestrates atmosphere, messages, voice, dice, and game mechanics
onto players' phones during a session.

## What we collect, and why
- **Player name + a device identifier** — to recognize you across reconnects within
  a campaign (no account/password). Stored on the realtime server.
- **Text/voice messages you send the GM** — when you use the quill (text) or the
  crystal-ball push-to-talk (voice). **Voice is captured only while you hold the
  button**, with a visible recording indicator, and is transcribed to text.
- **Room audio (GM-initiated)** — if the GM enables session capture, the GM's device
  records the table's audio (which may include your voice) and it is transcribed for
  the session log. **You see a recording indicator on your phone whenever this is
  active**, and it is disclosed at join. It is never captured silently.
- **Session logs/summaries** — transcripts and AI-written summaries of the session,
  and the chronicles delivered to you (your log history).
- **Device viewport size** — to lay out the GM's live stage; never screen contents.

We do **not** access your camera, contacts, location, browser history, or saved
credentials, and we do **not** sell personal data.

## Third-party processors
Audio/AI features send the relevant content to these providers, under their terms:
- **ElevenLabs** — speech-to-text (your voice clips / room audio) and text-to-speech.
- **Anthropic (Claude)** — session summaries and AI "agent" replies (your transcript
  / the GM's prompts).
These are used only to provide the features above. API keys are held server-side and
never exposed to clients.
